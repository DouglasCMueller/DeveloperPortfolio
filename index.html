Doug Mueller is a strategic financial expert skilled at driving business growth and profitability within public and private sectors. With over 15 years of industry experience, including regulatory compliance, relationship building, policy and procedure creation, and employee management, he blends sharp financial acumen with an exceptional leadership ability to deliver impactful outcomes. As a General Partner at Slainte Group LLC, Doug directs business administration and management efforts to support a variety of services, from venture and private equity to public investing. Managing a $1M venture investment portfolio, he leverages deep knowledge of emerging finance techniques and best practices to source and assess viable opportunities. Previously, he oversaw proprietary trading of a $25M syndicate book as Managing Director & Head of Trading at Andor Capital LP. As Principal & Head of Technology Trading at Pequot Capital, Doug executed trading processes for a $20B fund, including the equities, derivatives, and foreign exchange of a technology portfolio. Doug excels at forging and sustaining positive relationships with individuals and groups at all levels. At Andor Capital LP, he promoted a collaborative environment while coordinating global 24-hour trading desk functions with 12 traders, reaching $100M in annual sales commissions. He mentored junior traders, coaching on skills critical to meet stakeholder needs in a demanding and fast-paced environment. Doug holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting & Finance from Fairfield University and is currently completing a full stack web development course from the University of Connecticut School of Engineering. Residing in New Canaan, Connecticut with his wife and four children, he serves as a youth sports trainer, coach, and mentor helping young athletes reach success on the field, in the classroom, and in the workforce.
